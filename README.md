# 🎌 Lexi Subs

### Anime Subtitle Overlay & Japanese Study Tool

**Watch anime and learn Japanese at the same time — directly in your browser, on any streaming site.**

Lexi Subs overlays subtitles on top of any video player and transforms every subtitle line into an interactive language-learning experience.

Hover or click any word to instantly view readings, definitions, JLPT levels, and common-word information without leaving the video.

No tab switching. No copy-pasting. Just watch and learn.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🎌 Universal Subtitle Overlay

Load and display subtitles on virtually any video:

* `.srt`, `.vtt`, and `.ass` support
* Works on streaming sites and local videos
* Fully customizable:

  * Font
  * Size
  * Position
  * Colors
  * Outline / stroke

---

### 🔍 Built-in Subtitle Search & Download

Find subtitles without leaving the page.

Supports:

* Jimaku
* OpenSubtitles

Search, download, and load subtitles directly inside the extension.

---

### 📖 Instant Dictionary Lookup

Click or hover any Japanese word to open a dictionary popup.

See:

* Reading (Kana)
* English definitions
* JLPT level
* Common-word indicators

Powered by Jisho.

---

### 🧠 Intense Study Mode

Turn every subtitle line into a mini vocabulary lesson.

Words from the current subtitle appear below the video as study cards containing:

* Reading
* Definition
* Part of speech

Perfect for active immersion and sentence mining.

---

### ✅ Exception Words

Already know a word?

Mark it as known and Lexi Subs will automatically fade it out, helping you focus on vocabulary that actually matters.

---

### 📚 Custom Dictionary Support

Bring your own vocabulary database.

* Import `.tsv` dictionaries
* Supplement Jisho lookups
* Or completely replace them

Great for:

* Specialized terminology
* Anime-specific vocabulary
* Personal study decks

---

### ⏱️ Precision Subtitle Sync

Fine-tune subtitle timing with millisecond-level control.

Quick adjustments:

* ±1 ms
* ±10 ms
* ±100 ms

Perfect for fixing poorly synced subtitle files.

---

### 🔁 Auto Next-Episode Support

Binge-watch without reloading subtitles.

Lexi Subs automatically carries your subtitle setup into the next episode when supported.

---

### 🌐 Works Everywhere

Compatible with virtually any video player, including:

* Netflix
* Crunchyroll
* YouTube
* Local video files
* Most custom HTML5 video players
